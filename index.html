Vitus Sommet

What is it?

The All New Sommet - Better in every sense of the word. Improved suspension kinematics, geometry and an all new carbon frameset ready to race across the roughest enduro trails.

Our all-new Sommet is a 170mm big hitting enduro bike featuring a redesigned carbon front triangle and aluminium rear end. From alpine descents to technical trails, it offers better geometry, suspension, and seat post insertion for longer droppers. If you want a confidence-inspiring Enduro bike, the Sommet is for you.
